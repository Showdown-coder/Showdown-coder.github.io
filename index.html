Our Story

Quiz Informals was born during the unprecedented times of the COVID-19 pandemic when the world shifted to virtual spaces. What started as a way to keep minds engaged and spirits high during lockdown has evolved into a thriving community of knowledge enthusiasts.

What We Do

We create and curate engaging quiz content across various topics, fostering learning through fun and interactive sessions. Our quizzes range from general knowledge and current affairs to specialized themes, ensuring there's something for everyone.

Our Format Includes:

  • Written quizzes with detailed explanations
  • Interactive online sessions
  • Theme-based special editions
  • Collaborative community contributions
